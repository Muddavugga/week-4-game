Instructions
The force will provide you with a random number at the start of the game.There are four members of the dark side below. Each one will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your score to "Total Score" which is selected at random. You lose the game if your total score goes above that number. The value of member of the dark side is hidden from you until you click on him. The values will change at the start of eacn new game. May the force be with you.
